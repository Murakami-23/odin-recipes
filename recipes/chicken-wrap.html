Chicken-wrap

Description

This delicious recipe is not only finger-licking delicious but also very healthy. With more than enough greens included in the wrap you can be sure that you're getting a handful of nutrition in your daily diet. We all should know by now that chicken is one of the best sources of protein available to us. And the grilled chicken in this recipe is not only packed with protein but also delicious.

This recipe is quick and easy to make, taking up only an hour of your day to procure 6 of these delicious wraps. Sure to satisfy any child or adult's apppetite. The wrap can also be eaten as lunch or dinner. And you can bet that it is way better than take-out, both in taste and financially.

Ingredients

  • 3 Defrosted chicken breasts
  • Fresh lettuce
  • 5ml Cooking oil
  • Mayaonaisse
  • 6 Medium Burrito wraps
  • 2 Large tomatoes
  • 1 Large red onion

Steps

  1. Peel and cut up the onion, without crying, and set it aside
  2. Wash and cut up the tomatoes. Setting aside with the onions
  3. Wash and shred the lettuce and set aside
  4. Cut up your chicken breasts into 6 equal pieces and spice them according to your liking
  5. Heat up a pan and pour in the oil
  6. Grill the chicken until it turns brown and its not pink inside
  7. After chicken is done, lay out Burrito-wraps and smear with sufficient amount of Mayaonaisse
  8. Firstly add your lettuce followed by the tomatoes and then add your onion, lastly adding your grilled chicken on top
  9. Fold your, now stuffed, Burrito-wrap and enjoy
